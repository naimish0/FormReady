# FormReady privacy policy

Effective date: Publication date to be inserted when the hosted policy goes live.

FormReady prepares files locally on your Android device. No account is required and FormReady does
not operate a custom backend.

## Files and local data

Files you select are staged in app-private, no-backup storage for bounded local processing. The app
may keep local job history, settings, output metadata, custom presets and optional thumbnails
according to controls shown in the app. Originals are not overwritten. You choose if and where an
output is saved or shared; that destination may be a third-party or cloud-backed provider governed
by its own policy.

You can clear app history and temporary content, delete app-owned outputs through History, or clear
all app data through Android settings. Removing app history does not remove a file you explicitly
exported elsewhere.

## Network and third parties

FormReady has no custom backend, advertising, cloud storage, remote AI or Billing. Enhanced
document scanning and bundled OCR use Google ML Kit. Google states that document images and OCR
results are processed on-device, while the SDK may contact Google for module delivery, fixes,
model/accelerator compatibility, and diagnostic or usage metrics. The final Play Data Safety form
must reflect Google’s current ML Kit disclosure.

Optional ID-photo guidance and background replacement also use bundled ML Kit face detection and
person segmentation. FormReady does not identify people, compare faces, create embeddings, or
persist face landmarks or segmentation masks.

Supported PDF page operations use the local Apache-licensed PdfBox-Android engine. It has no
network capability and does not upload document content.

## Security and retention

Backup and cleartext traffic are disabled. Selected content is staged in private storage with
bounded streaming. Temporary and failed work follows explicit cleanup rules. Privacy Mode can
obscure sensitive previews in recents/background. No system can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

## Children and affiliation

The intended Play target audience is 13+ and must be confirmed truthfully in Play Console before
release. FormReady is an independent productivity utility and is not affiliated with any
government, examination body, employer or application portal.
