# FormReady privacy policy

Effective draft date: 23 July 2026

FormReady is designed to prepare files locally on your Android device. No account is required and FormReady does not operate a custom backend.

## Files and local data

When file workflows are enabled, a file you select is staged in app-private, no-backup storage for local processing. The app may keep local job history, settings, output metadata, and optional thumbnails according to controls shown in the app. Originals are not overwritten. You choose if and where an output is saved or shared; that destination may be a third-party or cloud-backed provider governed by its own policy.

You can clear app history and temporary content through app controls when those features are delivered, or clear all app data through Android settings. Removing app history does not necessarily remove a file you explicitly exported elsewhere.

## Network and third parties

The current Phase 0 build has no internet permission, advertising SDK, analytics, cloud storage, remote AI, or Billing. If a future release enables ads, consent, or Billing, this policy and the in-app disclosures must be updated before release.

## Security and retention

Backup is disabled, cleartext traffic is disabled, and selected content is staged in private storage with bounded streaming. Temporary and failed work is subject to cleanup rules implemented with each processor. No system can be guaranteed perfectly secure.

## Children and affiliation

The intended target audience decision will be finalized before Play release. FormReady is an independent productivity utility and is not affiliated with any government, examination body, employer, or application portal.
