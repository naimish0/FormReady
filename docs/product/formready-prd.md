# FormReady product requirements

FormReady is an independent Android utility that prepares photos, signatures, and PDFs against requirements selected by the user. It reports only measurable outcomes: **Ready**, **Ready with warnings**, or **Not Ready**. It never promises portal acceptance or official affiliation.

## Users and outcomes

Applicants, students, travellers, and document-service operators need to understand requirements expressed in pixels, physical dimensions, DPI, formats, page constraints, and decimal or binary file sizes. A successful workflow preserves the original, processes locally, validates the actual reopened export, and lets the user save or share it explicitly.

## Launch scope

Launch phases deliver: photo preparation; signature preparation; PDF inspection and honest compression modes; generic and source-verified presets; local history and settings; accessibility and localization; and release/policy work.

Scanner/OCR, ID-photo automation, batch processing, and Billing are post-v1 and must not appear as unfinished launch features.

## Product principles

- Offline core processing with no account or custom backend.
- Exact values and visible unit interpretation.
- No silent format conversion, distortion, PDF flattening, or original overwrite.
- Clear recovery for unsupported input, low storage, cancellation, and lost provider access.
- Accessibility at 200% font scale and with non-gesture alternatives.
- No government marks, official language, or guaranteed-acceptance claims.
