# Store listing draft

**Title:** FormReady: Photo & PDF

**Short description:** Prepare photos, signatures and PDFs for exact upload requirements.

## Full description

FormReady helps you prepare files for upload limits before you submit them. Enter or choose measurable requirements, prepare the file privately on your device, and check the actual exported result.

Planned launch capabilities include exact photo dimensions and file size, signature cleanup without stretching, honest PDF validation and compatibility compression, reusable presets, and clear Ready / warning / Not Ready reports.

Your files are processed on your device. No account is required. A file leaves the app only when you choose a save or share destination.

FormReady is an independent file-preparation utility and is not affiliated with any government, examination body, employer, or application portal. Always confirm the destination’s current requirements.
